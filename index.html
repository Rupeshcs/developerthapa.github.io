TO QUANTUM LEAP CLOUD ADOPTION IN INDIA

CREATING A COLLABORATIVE APPROACH FOR A NATIONWIDE IMPACT

Aspiring to emerge as one of the fastest-growing data economies, India's adoption of cloud technologies will be imperative to tap into the global cloud market, which is expected to grow to $832.1 billion by 2025. To help enterprises and the industry embrace this opportunity and further the #DigitalIndia agenda, NASSCOM is launching the India Cloud Advocacy Program (ICAP) to engage with the Indian cloud ecosystem through national conferences, regional connects, research papers, advocacy, amongst others.

The inaugural edition of the NASSCOM Cloud Summit, with the theme 'Unlocking Value From Cloud: Empowering India's Digital Journey' is one such effort under the ICAP that will bring together various stakeholders from the ecosystem - government, supply & demand side enterprises, SMEs, global thought leaders, analysts and SMBs - to discuss some of the challenges and their solutions towards cloud adoption in the country.
